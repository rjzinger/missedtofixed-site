Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 31, 2026

This page is a pre-sale page for a planned children's bedtime-story service. It exists so we can learn whether families want what we're building. This policy explains what we collect on this page and how we handle it. It's written plainly on purpose.

Built for parents, not marketed to children

This page is intended for parents and caregivers. We do not knowingly collect any information from children, and we do not ask for any information about a child on this page — no names, ages, or interests. We only interact with the adult who fills out the form.

What we collect

If you join the waitlist, we collect:

  • Your email address (required), so we can send you a sample story and updates.
  • Your first name (optional), only to address you warmly.

If you reserve a founding spot, payment is handled entirely by Stripe, our payment processor. We never see or store your full card details; Stripe does, under its own privacy policy.

We also use privacy-respecting analytics to count visits and which buttons get clicked, in aggregate. We do not use this to build advertising profiles of you.

How we use it

  • To send you a free sample story and occasional updates about the launch.
  • To process and manage a founding reservation, if you make one.
  • To understand, in aggregate, whether this idea is worth building.

What we don't do

  • We never sell or rent your information.
  • We don't share it except with the service providers that make this work (our email tool and Stripe), and only as needed.
  • We don't collect any data about your child on this page.

Your choices

You can unsubscribe from emails at any time using the link in any message. You can ask us to delete your information at any time — just email us and we'll take care of it.

Contact
